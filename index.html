About Rova

A scooter company built for cities that have seen the rest.

Rova is a Canberra-based shared e-scooter company preparing to launch in the ACT and NSW. We're building a compliance-first operation — using AI-assisted parking validation, real-time fleet monitoring, and best-in-class hardware — to deliver a service that cities actually want to keep.

Compliance-first

Built around AI-assisted parking, real-time fleet monitoring, and the Segway Pilot Lite safety system.

Sustainable by design

Zero-emission fleet. Charging from renewables. Designed to reduce car trips, not just replace walking.

For the greater good

We exist to make cities better — cleaner, less congested, and more connected.

The Vision

Scooters are the beginning, not the destination.

Every great platform starts with a single use case. Ours starts with shared mobility — and builds toward something far more ambitious.

Phase 1 — Today

Shared E-Scooters

Rova launches as a shared e-scooter operator — giving city residents a fast, affordable, and sustainable way to get around. Every scooter in our fleet is GPS-tracked, compliance-monitored, and designed to meet the highest regulatory standards.

  • Segway Apex D110L hardware
  • AI-assisted parking validation
  • Atom Mobility fleet platform
  • Real-time compliance monitoring
Phase 2 — Tomorrow

Autonomous Last-Mile Delivery

Our long-term vision goes beyond ride-sharing. We are developing a platform where the same vehicles that carry riders today will autonomously run errands, deliver groceries, and move goods across the city tomorrow — reducing delivery vans, cutting emissions, and making urban logistics smarter.

  • Autonomous navigation platform
  • Last-mile goods delivery
  • Reduced urban freight emissions
  • Smarter city logistics
